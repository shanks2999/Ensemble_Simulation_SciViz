Info:

  • Use mouse click & drag to rotate the cylinder.
  • Use mouse scroll to zoom in and zoom out.
  • Use keys(A/D) to move the XY 3D-rectangular plane left & right.
  • Use keys(W/S) to move the XZ 3D-rectangular plane up & down.
  • Once you hit the keys (W, A, S, D) all particles inside the 3-D cylinder will be greyed out.
    Only particles which fall inside the 3D-rectangle(XY & XZ) will show the color concentration
    The 2-D view on the right will show the particles which fall inside both the rectangles.
